Help and Options

This is where the interview will take place. When you are ready, generate a new question, and you will be given prep time to prepare your answer. Once the timer is up, the interview will begin. Once the timer for the interview runs out, your video will be saved and graded.

Here you will find links to websites which provide advice for preparing for interviews, and tips on acing and structuring your interviews.

Upon completion, an AI will process and evaluate your interview and grade it based on several categories, and you can see these results in this page! With these stats, reflect and try and improve your strategy before trying again.
