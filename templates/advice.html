One-way video interviews where you record responses to fixed questions. Typically **2-5 questions** from three categories:

  • Motivational: Why this industry? Why this company?
  • Competency: Discuss a recent transaction or macroeconomic trend.
  • Behavioural: Tell me about a time when you faced a challenge and what you learned.

You'll typically have 30 seconds - 2 minutes of prep and 1 - 3 minutes to answer.

  • Company Research - Study their reports, values, and recent deals.
  • Attend Insight Days - Follow their LinkedIn & Bright Network.
  • Use Mergersight - Analyze past M&A transactions.
  • Read Financial News - Financial Times, The Economist.
  • Practice Storytelling - Use the STAR method for responses.

Structure responses using STAR:

  • Situation: Provide context (where, when, what).
  • Task: What did you need to achieve?
  • Action: Explain how you solved the problem.
  • Result: Describe the outcome and what you learned.

Example:

Situation: Led a team project with a tight deadline.
Task: Deliver a client proposal in five days.
Action: Assigned tasks based on strengths and held daily check-ins.
Result: Submitted ahead of time, and the client approved our work.
